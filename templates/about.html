ABOUT US



We are an online platform for renting medium and long-term properties that seeks to simplify the leasing process in Colombia. We aim to rent with just one click, avoiding the need for visits to properties and bureaucratic procedures.





WHAT MAKES US DIFFERENT FROM OTHERS?

We have a highly qualified team that verifies properties and their tenants, offering a 100% digital rental alternative that guarantees an easy and efficient solution for all types of audiences (students, foreigners, and professionals), without so much bureaucracy.




HOW DOES IT WORK?

1. Start the search process through our platform: indicate parameters such as budget, location and general characteristics of the property.

2. Reservation: our team will be in charge of validating the information of both the applicant and the property to be rented.

3. Approval: in a maximum of 2 days, the applicant will be given a response informing the rental feasibility under the previously established parameters.

4. Payment.

5. Delivery: once the payment has been received and due verification by the O-Hom team and the owner, the property is delivered.
